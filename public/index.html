Event Horizon

Explore your area's most intersting events

Find Events!

Event Horizon is a platform for finding events all around the world. Its database of events is open for anyone to browse, and with just a username and password to pick, you can become a member and follow events, rate hosting organizations, and direct message those same hosts with any questions.

Starting your own event

Event planners can create organizations which they can post events from, then use real Google Maps data to show their users exactly where in the world the event is. To become an event planner, just check the event planner box during sign-up or if you forget, go to your account and check the box from there.

And More...

  • Post Pictures! Once you have created your event, go to that event's edit page to add any pictures you think will showcase why people should come!

  • Follow events to have them displayed on your profile page for easy finding later!

  • Is event planning your profession? Open up a different organization for each client, and when you recieve messages from potential event goers, they will be labeled with the organization they are responding for!
